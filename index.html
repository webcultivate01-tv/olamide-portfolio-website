FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me and my design process. Can't find your answer? Get in touch.

I follow a strategic, collaborative approach broken into four key phases:

1. Discovery:

Deep dive into your business, audience, competitors, and goals. I ask the right questions to understand your vision.

2. Strategy:

Define positioning, messaging, and visual direction. This foundation ensures every design decision serves your goals.

3. Design:

Create concepts, refine through feedback, and build comprehensive brand systems ready to scale.

4. Launch:

Deliver all assets, brand guidelines, and support to ensure smooth implementation across touchpoints.

Timeline varies based on scope and complexity:

Logo Design: 3-4 weeks
Brand Identity: 4-6 weeks
Complete System: 6-10 weeks
Campaign Design: 2-4 weeks

I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific needs and requirements.

Pricing is custom based on your project scope, complexity, and timeline. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all pricing.

What I consider:

  • • Project scope and deliverables
  • • Timeline and urgency
  • • Revision rounds needed
  • • Industry and market positioning
  • • Long-term partnership potential

Let's schedule a consultation to discuss your project and I'll provide a detailed, transparent quote.
