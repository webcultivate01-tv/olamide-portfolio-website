Project Overview

Nova Tech is a B2B SaaS platform that helps enterprises manage their data infrastructure. They approached us to completely rebrand their identity as they prepared for Series A funding.

The challenge was to create a modern, trustworthy brand that appeals to enterprise clients while maintaining the innovation and agility that defines their product.

The Challenge

  • • Outdated brand perception in a competitive market
  • • Need to appeal to enterprise decision-makers
  • • Establish credibility and trust
  • • Create a scalable visual system

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive brand system that balances sophistication with approachability. The new identity features a modern geometric logo, refined color palette, and flexible typography system.

Logo Design

A modern geometric mark that represents data flow and connectivity.

Color System

A refined palette that conveys trust, innovation, and professionalism.
