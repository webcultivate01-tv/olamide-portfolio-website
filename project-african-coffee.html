Case Study

African Coffee Co.

Modern packaging design inspired by rich African heritage and coffee culture.

Year

2023

Duration

4 Weeks

Category

Concept Work

Project Overview

African Coffee Co. celebrates the rich heritage of African coffee production. They needed packaging design that honored their roots while appealing to modern, conscious consumers.

We created a design system that tells the story of African coffee farmers and the journey from farm to cup.

The Challenge

  • • Honor African heritage authentically
  • • Stand out on retail shelves
  • • Communicate premium quality and sustainability
  • • Create emotional connection with consumers

Our Solution

We developed a packaging system featuring traditional African patterns, earthy color palettes, and storytelling elements. Each package tells the story of a specific coffee origin and the farmers behind it.

Visual Language

Traditional patterns combined with modern typography for authentic yet contemporary design.

Storytelling

Each package includes QR codes linking to farmer stories and origin information.
