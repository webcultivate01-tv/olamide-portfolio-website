Case Study

Lumina Wellness

Designing a serene brand identity for a wellness startup that guides users toward mindful living through calming visuals and intuitive experiences.

Year

2023

Duration

5 Weeks

Category

Brand Identity

Project Overview

Lumina Wellness is a holistic wellness platform offering meditation, yoga, and mindfulness coaching. They needed a brand identity that conveyed calm, clarity, and transformation.

We created a serene, modern brand system that reflects the peace and balance at the core of their mission.

The Challenge

  • • Differentiate in a saturated wellness market
  • • Convey calm and professionalism simultaneously
  • • Create a brand that feels both modern and timeless
  • • Build trust with health-conscious consumers

Our Solution

We developed a serene brand system featuring soft, flowing shapes, a calming color palette, and elegant typography. The identity balances minimalism with warmth.

Brand Mark

A flowing, organic mark that represents growth, balance, and inner light.

Digital Experience

A cohesive design system for app and web that guides users through their wellness journey.
