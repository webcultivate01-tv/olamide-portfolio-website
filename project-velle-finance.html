Case Study

Velle Finance

Launch campaign for a fintech platform making investing accessible to everyone.

Year

2024

Duration

3 Weeks

Category

Campaign Design

Project Overview

Velle Finance is a fintech platform democratizing investment opportunities for everyday people. They needed a bold launch campaign that communicated accessibility, trust, and innovation.

Our campaign positioned Velle as the friendly, intelligent alternative to traditional finance.

The Challenge

  • • Break through a crowded fintech market
  • • Build trust with first-time investors
  • • Create urgency around platform launch
  • • Communicate complex financial concepts simply

Our Solution

We created a multi-channel campaign featuring bold typography, relatable messaging, and a cohesive visual system. The campaign emphasized simplicity and accessibility across all touchpoints.

Campaign Strategy

Positioned Velle as the accessible, intelligent choice for modern investors.

Creative Assets

Digital ads, social content, and landing pages with consistent messaging.

Results

5K+

Sign-ups in first week
